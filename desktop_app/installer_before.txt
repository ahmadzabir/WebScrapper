Windows SmartScreen / Antivirus Notice
=====================================

This app is not code-signed. Windows may show a "Windows protected your PC" or SmartScreen warning when you first run it.

What to do:
1. Click "More info" on the warning
2. Click "Run anyway"

Or: Right-click the shortcut > Properties > check "Unblock" at the bottom > OK

The app is open-source. You can inspect the code at:
https://github.com/ahmadzabir/WebScrapper
